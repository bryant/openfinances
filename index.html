    Option Pricing with Python

    Fame isn't all

    The Black-Sholes-Merton model is very popular but that model isn't actually being used by options traders . In fact, they mostly use a heuristically derived pricing formula which is then adapted by varying the standard deviation of a Gaussian. Studying the in and outs of the Black Scholes Model isn't without interest though .
